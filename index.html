How to play

  • The goal of the game is to guess the Weapon of the Day based on the stats and matches found throughout the game.
  • For every guess, each cell will appear as green, yellow, or red depending on if the specific value matches.
  • Green means the stat matches with the WOTD.
  • Yellow (only for ability) means the guessed weapon shares only one ability (if either the weapon guessed or the WOTD have 2 abilities).
  • Red means that the stat is wrong. For Damage and Strength, there will be arrows (↑/↓) indicating whether the guessed weapon's stat needs to be higher or lower to match the WOTD's corresponding stat.

Clarifications

  • The only Categories included are Sword, Bow, Longsword and Gauntlet.
  • Weapons with the "Wand" category (or "offensive wands") are not included, since this category is mostly for support items. Skyblock is very inconsistent, so only wands in the "Sword" category are included.
    (To see a list of weapons this excludes, check here.)
  • Items who have damage stats but aren't considered weapons (Fishing rods, Pickaxes, Axes...) are not included.
  • Weapons that drop directly from dungeon clear enemies, Daedalus Blade, and Great Spook Sword are not included, since they have 0 stats by default.
  • Skyblock versions of Vanilla weapons are not included.
  • Abilities that aren't indicated by "Ability: " in the item's description are not counted.
    An exception to this are the Wither Blades, these are listed as Right click.
  • Abilities without an activation, "On Shoot" or "On Hit" are all considered passive for consistency.
  • Since the Blaze Slayer daggers come in pairs with identical stats, these are grouped into "T1/T2/T3 Blaze Daggers". If you've guessed one of them, you won't be able to guess the other one.
    • Any other cases of items with identical stats are counted separately, so make sure to pay attention to the alert box.
